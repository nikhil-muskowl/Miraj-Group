Starting with a simple beginning in year 1987, today MIRAJ Group has created its presence in various sectors. As years passed MIRAJ established itself as a big business empire with a consolidation of various sectors.

In its initial years MIRAJ was committed to the tobacco business. MIRAJ provided superior quality of lime processed chewing tobacco, a pioneer quality economy product. Gradually the group achieved new heights. Starting with single tobacco division today MIRAJ has entered into various segments such as Beauty care, Safety matches, Stationary, Plastics, Pipes & fittings to Hospitality sector, Engineering Division, Perfumery Division ,the Tea sector , Music Industry, Film Making division . Today MIRAJ group is one of the most admired business houses of the region extending its arm towards remaining regions of the country.
