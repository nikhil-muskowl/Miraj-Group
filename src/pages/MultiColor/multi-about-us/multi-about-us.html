PROFILE

MIRAJ MULTICOLOUR PVT. LTD IS A STATIONERY, PRINTING AND PACKAGING SOLUTION PROVIDER TO ALL ITS CUSTOMERS.

Miraj Multicolour Pvt. Ltd. is an ISO 9001:2008 Certified Company. We entered this domain in the year 2001, under the remarkable leadership of Mr. K. G. Sharma, director of the company. Because of their diligent efforts and motivational skills we managed to carve a niche for our organization as one of the reputed Office Stationery Products manufacturing, supplying from Udaipur and exporter in all over world. The credit for our success also goes to our wide range of products that includes Spiral Books, Double Ruled Paper, Practical Notebooks, Pocket Memo, and so on that caters the various stationery requirements of schools and offices.
Complete with hi-tech technology, huge infrastructure and more importantly we have a gem of people works 24x7 to achieve utmost satisfaction to the customers. We have experts employees related to pre-press, press, post-press and quality control.

MIRAJ is providing complete commercial print services from Pre-press, Priting and Post-press. We are mastered in essential products like corporate brochures, catalogues, mono cartons, leaflets, danglers, fmcg & pharmaceutical packaging products, annual reports, calenders, exercise Books and many more..

We enjoy transforming your vision into the final product, and have the experience and repertoire of tools to make it happen. More importantly, our team has not just the skills, but also the attitude and enthusiasm to bring out the best product
